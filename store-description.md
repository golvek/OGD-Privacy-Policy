## Ogden – Task & Time Tracker for Raters

A lightweight, always-visible overlay that helps raters track their tasks and time without getting in the way.

**How it works**

A small floating widget sits on your screen showing your task count, accumulated time, and a countdown timer. It reads the page to detect when you submit tasks and automatically tracks everything – no clicking around, no manual logging.

**Features**

- **Automatic task counting** – Detects submissions and increments your count
- **Countdown timer** – Configurable timer (default 90s) that ticks down while you're working on a task
- **Dual mode support** – Switch between two task types with one click, or let it auto-detect
- **Drag to reposition** – Move the overlay anywhere on screen
- **Daily reset** – Automatically resets counters at your configured hour
- **History page** – Review your daily metrics with task counts and total time per mode
- **Clean, minimal design** – Small footprint, dark theme, stays out of your way

**Privacy**

Ogden is a watcher – it reads the page to detect submissions but never modifies, injects, or interacts with page content. No data is sent anywhere. All settings and history stay on your device.

**All features free.** No ads, no signups, no premium tiers.
